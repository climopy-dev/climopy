About the authors

Main authors
------------
* `Luke Davis`_

Bios
----

`Luke Davis`_ is the sole developer as of January 2021. He is a graduate student in
climate science at Colorado State University. Inspired by `metpy
<https://unidata.github.io/MetPy/latest/index.html>`__, a package used for
meteorological calculations and visualizations, he aimed to create a package suitable
for the statistical and objective analysis tasks required by dynamicists and climate
scientists. He is also the lead developer of `ProPlot
<https://github.com/lukelbd/proplot>`__, a tool for making high-quality plots in
matplotlib.


.. _Luke Davis: https://github.com/lukelbd
